BIG BANG

Since their debut in 2005, "SUPER JUNIOR" has gained international recognition not only in Asia but all over the world—including countries in North, South, and Central America, Europe, and the Middle East, and the group continues to lead the Korean wave with their reputation as talented entertainers. SUPER JUNIOR is comprised not only of singers, but of highly-regarded actors, MCs, comedians, composers and models.

SUPER JUNIOR became a national phenomenon sweeping multiple music awards and also proved themselves internationally. To date, the group has released over 30 albums in both Korea and Japan, including their most successful album "Sorry, Sorry" along with fan-favorites "Mr. Simple" and "Sexy, Free & Single".

Furthermore, the group has proven their staying power, performing a long-standing concert series titled "SUPER SHOW". SUPER JUNIOR has held more than 140 concerts in 30 cities around the world, in Asia, Europe, and the Americas, with cumulative audience numbers totaling over 2 million.

And in March 2021, SUPER JUNIOR made their long-awaited comeback with their 10th full-length album "The Renaissance". The album achieved No. 1 on the iTunes Top Album Charts in 23 countries, also topping China's QQ Music, Kugou Music, and Kuwo Music's Digital Album Sales Charts, once again showcasing the longevity of their career and prominent international presence throughout the years.

"SHINee" is a contemporary band that sets and leads trends in all areas including music, dance, and fashion. The team has continuously set records as a global K-Pop leader with their outstanding singing, performance, and trendy music style.

Starting with their solo concert in 2011, the first ever in London, England by a Korean idol group, SHINee's popularity expanded beyond the borders of Asia into North and Central America, picking up momentum through their world tour. In 2013, the team ranked No.10 on the U.S. Billboard "2013 Year End Chart", becoming the first Korean group to earn such a title.

In February, SHINee made a much-anticipated comeback after 2 years and 6 months with their 7th full-length album "Don't Call Me". The album reached No.1 on iTunes Top Album chart in 47 regions globally, also topping Korean music charts in addition to China's QQ Music and Kugou Music's Digital Album Sales charts, Japan's Oricon Weekly Digital Album Chart, LINE Music Album Top 100 Chart and Billboard Japan's Weekly Top Album Sales chart, once again proving their staying power and global popularity. SHINee held their first-ever online concert "Beyond LIVE - SHINee : SHINee WORLD" this April gathering high level of anticipation worldwide.

BIGBANG who produced numerous hit songs since their debut in 2006, has now comeback with "Still Life" in about 4 years since their last single "FLOWER ROAD". The song expresses BIGBANG's past and portrays their genuine message as a group that influenced the overall popular culture over the past years with their music, fashion, and choreography. "Still Life" will be forever remembered as a song that comforts and touches the hearts of many people.
